Privacy Statement

Effective date: June 24, 2023

This Privacy Statement is issued by Purpose-Quest, operating the website www.purpose-quest.com (hereinafter "we", "our", "us"). This statement explains how we, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), handle the personal data of our users (hereinafter "you", "your").

What Data Do We Collect?

Purpose Quest collects the following data:

  • Personal identification information such as name, email address, and locale for users signing in via Google integration on Auth0.
  • Google user ID, profile picture, and social sign-in data.
  • Login details like IP address, login time, and login count.
  • Email address and verification status for users signing up with email and password.
  • Auth0 user ID and login details for email sign-ups.

How Do We Collect Your Data?

Your data is collected directly when you:

  • Sign up for our service using Google integration or email and password via Auth0.
  • Voluntarily share your story within our products.

How Will We Use Your Data?

Purpose Quest uses your data solely for app functionality, enabling you to access and use our services effectively.

How Do We Store Your Data?

Your login data is stored securely by Auth0, adhering to their Data Privacy and Compliance policies.

Your story data is stored in a Supabase database, encrypted using Fernet encryption. For more details, refer to Supabase's Privacy Policy.

Payments are processed via Stripe, and related data is handled according to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Marketing

Purpose Quest does not use your data for marketing purposes.

What Are Your Data Protection Rights?

You have various data protection rights, including:

  • The right to access, rectify, or erase your data.
  • The right to restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability.

To exercise these rights, please email info@purpose-quest.com.

Cookies

We use functional session-related cookies, managed by our server, containing only your account ID.

Privacy Policies of Other Websites

Our service may link to other websites. Please note that our privacy policy applies only to our website. Other websites have their own privacy policies.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We regularly review and update our privacy policy. Changes are not proactively communicated but are agreed upon during each product access.

How to Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, you can contact us at info@purpose-quest.com.

How to Contact the Appropriate Authorities

In the Netherlands, you can contact the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens for any data protection concerns. For more information, visit their contact page.
